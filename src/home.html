What is LGBTQ community?

"Openness may not completely disarm prejudice, but it's a good place to start." - Jason Collins

The LGBTQ community is a vibrant and diverse collective of individuals whose sexual orientations and gender identities transcend societal norms. It encompasses people who identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and various other identities. What unites this community is a shared experience of navigating a world that often marginalizes and discriminates against non-heteronormative identities.
