Blue Diamond Society

Non-governmental organization

Kathmandu · 980-7522030

Address: P8HR+7X2, Dhumbharai Marg, Kathmandu 44600

The Blue Diamond Society is an LGBT rights organization in Nepal. It was established in 2001 to advocate for change in the existing laws against homosexuality and to advocate for the rights of Nepal's marginalized gay, transgender, and other sexual minority communities.

Mitini Nepal

Non-governmental organization

Phone: 977-01-4957590

Email: mitini.nepal@gmail.com

Address: Samakhushi - Ranibari Rd ward no. -26, Kathmandu 44600

Mitini Nepal (Mitini) works for lesbian, bisexual, transgender, queer and intersex + rights in Nepal and is the first established LBTQI+ organisation of Nepal.Since 2005, Mitini has been advocating for the inclusion of LBTQI+ women in The Women’s Movement and has been closely working with women-rights organisations.
